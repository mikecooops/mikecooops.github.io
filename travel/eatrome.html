Eat: Rome


"Arancini"

Risotto balls stuffed with mozzarella and peas, rolled in breadcrumbs
and deep fried. These are great on the go snacks and popular form of
street food across Rome.



"Carbonara"

Pasta, typically linguine, tossed in an egg and cheese base with prosciutto.
A simple yet delicious dish.*To see how to make your own carbonara,
click here*



"Roman Pizza"

Thin and crispy pizza made with fresh, local ingredients. Enough said.

Space Cadet – What lies beyond the great unknown? What sort of life might exist? How might we communicate with them if they are out there? Will it be through sound? Symbols? Movement? The unique typeface, “Space Cadet” is inspired by the mystery that is our universe. Each character is constructed with bold shapes that act as cut-outs. In addition, rigid lines help capture the essence of the letter without giving away too much. Space Cadet works well in the modern setting. Some label it as abstract. Abstract is defined as existing in thought or as an idea but not having a physical or concrete existence. Much of space is abstract, in that we speculate what might be out there yet we have no proof. Using this typeface for headlines rather than body text is sure to enhance any layout.
